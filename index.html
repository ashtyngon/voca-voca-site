Instant Lookup

Highlight any word on any webpage to see its definition, pronunciation, part of speech, and usage examples without leaving the page.

Smart Collection

Every word is saved with its original context and source. Organize with folders, track mastery levels, and search your entire library.

Spaced Repetition

A scientifically-proven algorithm schedules reviews at optimal intervals. Practice with flashcards and track your retention rate.

Audio Pronunciation

Hear every word pronounced correctly with auto-play audio. Learn how words sound, not just what they mean.

Full-Screen Mode

Use the side panel for quick lookups or switch to full-screen mode for focused practice sessions.

Cloud Sync

Sign in with Google and your vocabulary, progress, and stats sync instantly between Chrome and the iOS app.

Practice anywhere

Collect words on your computer while reading articles, research, or the news. Then pull out your phone and practice on the train, in line, or on the couch. Your entire collection and progress syncs automatically.
