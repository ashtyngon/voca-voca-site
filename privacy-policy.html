Voca-Voca Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 14, 2026

What Voca-Voca Does

Voca-Voca is a vocabulary learning extension that lets you look up word definitions while reading online, save words to a personal collection, and practice with spaced repetition.

Data We Collect

  • Google account info (email, display name) — used solely for sign-in and syncing your data across devices.
  • Words you save — the word, its definition, the sentence it appeared in, and the page URL where you found it.
  • Practice data — review history, mastery levels, and session statistics.
  • Extension settings — your preferences such as dark mode and practice options.

How We Store Data

Your data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API. If you sign in with Google, your data is also synced to Google Firebase Firestore, where it is stored securely under your unique user account and protected by Firebase security rules that prevent any other user from accessing it.

Third-Party Services

  • Free Dictionary API (api.dictionaryapi.dev) — to fetch word definitions. Only the looked-up word is sent.
  • Datamuse API (api.datamuse.com) — to fetch related words. Only the looked-up word is sent.
  • Wiktionary (en.wiktionary.org) — for additional definitions. Only the looked-up word is sent.
  • Google Firebase — for authentication and cloud sync of your personal data.

No other third-party services receive your data.

Payments

Voca-Voca may offer paid subscription plans. Payments are processed by Stripe (stripe.com). When you subscribe, your payment information (such as credit card details) is collected and processed directly by Stripe — Voca-Voca never sees, stores, or has access to your full payment details. We only receive a confirmation of your subscription status from Stripe. Stripe's privacy policy is available at stripe.com/privacy.

What We Do NOT Do

  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties.
  • We do not use your data for advertising or marketing.
  • We do not track your browsing activity beyond the specific words you choose to look up.
  • We do not use analytics or tracking scripts.
  • We do not store or have access to your payment card details — all payment processing is handled by Stripe.

Data Deletion

You can delete individual words or your entire collection at any time from within the extension. Uninstalling the extension removes all local data. To delete cloud-synced data, sign in and clear your collection before uninstalling, or contact us at the email below.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact support@voca-voca.app.
