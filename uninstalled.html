Voca-Voca was removed. Here's what happened to your vocabulary — and how to get it back.

If you signed in with Google: your data is safe

Every word you saved is backed up to your account in our cloud sync. Reinstall Voca-Voca, sign in with the same Google account, and your collection will return automatically within a few seconds.

If you never signed in: data was stored only on this browser

Without sign-in, your vocabulary lived in this Chrome installation's local storage and was removed with the extension. If you exported a backup file (CSV / Anki / JSON) from inside Voca-Voca before uninstalling, you can re-import it on reinstall.
